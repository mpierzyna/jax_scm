# GABLS1 validation
jax-scm is compared against the GABLS1 intercomparison results from Cuxart et al. (2006). The reference data is taken from the figures in the paper.

- Nz = 64 agrees very well
- Nz = 128 starts showing oscillation in Km and Kh; fluxes and mean profiles still good
- Nz = 400 show significant oscillations in Km and Kh; fluxes and mean profiles don't agree anymore
