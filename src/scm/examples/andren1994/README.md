# Andren et al. 1994 test case

This example implements a neutral test case proposed by Andren et al. (1994).
Initial conditions for velocity and TKE are taken from Tab A1 of the original paper.
The case has constant geostrophic forcing, a neutral stratification, and a constant Coriolis parameter.

**Reference**

> Andren, A., et al. "Large‐eddy Simulation of a Neutrally Stratified Boundary Layer: A Comparison of Four Computer Codes." 
> Quarterly Journal of the Royal Meteorological Society, vol. 120, no. 520, Oct. 1994, pp. 1457–84. https://doi.org/10.1002/qj.49712052003.
